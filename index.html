About

Hey there, and welcome to this corner of my site. I've decided to turn this page into a large resource, mainly of other resources so that you are never left unsure on where to look.

Websites

Much like this very one, there are many great websites already dedicated to teaching people the basics, supplying them with resources so that they can further themselves as well as more general things.

develop.games

develop.games is a website made and maintained by the amazing Thor over at Pirate Software. Thor often heavily emphasises that you don't need to be skilled to make games. You don't need to be a mastermind who can do anything and everything, all you need is determination and a bit of elbow grease. His site talks about everything, from how you don't need skills to make games (with great examples), financing and marketing your game, and even what to watch out for in the process of doing so. It also inspired me to do this site so go check it out.

w3schools

I can almost guarantee that you've come across w3schools at some point before. It's a great resource for anyone to learn to code from, reference, or maybe steal code from their examples. It covers a wide range of languages and even some job areas and what you need to know for them.

itch.io

While itch.io is predominantly a publishing platform, it has much more to offer. To start with, it has a huge range of assets which you are able to browse, there's even tools and software so that you can make your own. To add to this, there are also many community run game jams which is always great for boosting skills, finding flaws, and just a good time in general.

Dev Assets

Dev Assets is a site created by the team at Brackeys which hosts an assortment of free 2D and 3D game assets which you can use in your project.

Kenney

Kenney has created an incredible amount of assets, many of which are free. This ranges from 2D to 3D, audio to UI, and even tools so that you can make your own. And then to wrap it all up, they have an all-in-one package for every one of their nearly 40,000 assets at a cheap price. If you want a treasure trove of high quality assets, this is the place to go.
